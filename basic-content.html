Projects

  • Foodsharing Copenhagen

    Assignment Focus

    Utilising the skills developed throughout the first semester, this assignments focus was to develop a campaign site for Foodsharing (Copenhagen), to generate awareness towards their cause. This assignment included developing a campaign site, which would provide the receiver fulfil our objective of raising awareness surrounding food-waste.

    Assigned Role

    For this assignment, my role included coordinating activities, assisting in copywriting, recording voice-over assets and presenting the final concept to the client.

    Learning Outcome

    From this assignment I came to understand the importance of project and time management to meet deadlines. In addition, preparation and setting clear objectives allowed my team and I to complete the assignment. This is demonstrated through:

    • Identifying and targeting the appropriate audience through desk research of client and their given brief.

    • Creating a narrative based on the topic of food waste with a non-conventional, humorous appeal to generate greater awareness and improve the users experience.

    • Developing a campaign with a clear objective by utilising visual and audio cues.

  • Storyboard

    Assignment Focus

    Creating a storyboard enabled us to organise the scenes, ultimately delivering a better shoot. This was achieved through identify the message to be conveyed, then highlighting the key frames.

    Assigned Role

    During the development of the story board, our team collaborated in developing ideas and arranging the frames. Additionally, including information such as equipment and roles, improved the quality of the shoots.

    Learning Outcome

    During this process I came to understand the importance of composition and alternative methods of conveying a message. This included using

    • Non-verbal cues such as body language or perspective shots.
    • Providing a variety of shots and edits.
    • Utilising sound to emphasise particular points.

  • Customer Pitch

    Assignment Focus

    The customers pitch allowed my team and I present our campaign in a concise and effective way, as to convince the client of our campaign objective. This was achieved through presenting a visual deck, campaign page and video.

    Assigned Role

    My role included, in collaboration with the group, identifying a concise objective for the campaign and to clearly explain how to achieve our goal: to save the planet.

    Learning Outcome

    From the feedback gained after delivering the pitch, I learned the importance of clearly reading a brief and outlining the clients needs as well as presenting unique ideas, justified with research.
